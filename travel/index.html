Travelling To Singapore

Overview

Due to the COVID-19 pandemic, international travel (to Singapore) has been restricted. SafeTravel lanes are available to facilitate controlled entry to Singapore based on the categories of the travellers (i.e. Singaporeans, Singapore Permanent Residents, Long-Term Pass Holders, and International Travellers).

For checking prevailing travel conditions to Singapore, please visit the website for more information

https://safetravel.ica.gov.sg/arriving/overview

All travellers/residents in Singapore are required to wear a face mask at all times in public. From time to time, some conditions/guidelines are subjected to change for safe outdoor activities, for safe shopping, for safe dinning and for safe commuting. Please visit the website

https://www.gov.sg/article/covid-19-resources

To find out more about the COVID-19 situation in Singapore, please visit the website

https://www.moh.gov.sg/covid-19

Official COVID-19 Resource

For any COVID-19 related resources/information in Singapore, please visit https://www.gov.sg/article/covid-19-resources

Climate

Singapore has a tropical climate. Temperature ranges from a low of 24ºC to a high of around 31ºC every day, and relative humidity is high.

Currency Exchange

Singapore dollar (S$) is accepted at stores and restaurants. You can exchange your currency for S$ at foreign exchange banks and authorized money exchange offices.

Electricity

The electrical power supply is 230V at 50Hz. Three pin-sockets are the norm.

Shopping

The business hours of most department stores are from 10:00 to 22:00. Department stores and most tourist attraction places are open 7 days a week. For a safe shopping guidelines/conditions, please visit
https://www.gov.sg/article/covid-19-resources
